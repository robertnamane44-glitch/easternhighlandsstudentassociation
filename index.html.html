Our Activities

Celebrating culture, education, sports, service, and unity

Cultural Performances

We proudly showcase traditional dances and cultural heritage during school shows and university events.

Our dances symbolize unity and pride in Eastern Highlands traditions.

Event: DWU Cultural Show 2025 – Madang Campus

Career Expo

Our members participate in career expos, promoting Divine Word University across high schools in PNG.

Inspiring future leaders through education and awareness.

Event: Career Expo 2025 – Madang High School

Sports - East Nokondis

EHPSA supports the East Nokondis rugby league team, fostering teamwork and athletic excellence.

Strength, unity, and determination on the field.

Match: Interfaculty Rugby League 2025 – DWU Oval

Community Service

We engage in projects such as campus clean-ups and hospital visits to Modilon Hospital, showing our commitment to giving back.

Serving with compassion and responsibility for our community.

Event: Campus Clean-Up & Modilon Hospital Visit – 2025

Bilum Exchange

We host events where we invite other provincial student associations, exchange bilums, share stories, and build networks.

Strengthening bonds through cultural exchange and friendship.

Event: Bilum Exchange with Jiwaka Student Association – 2025
